Anchor case · monitoring & triage

A Brussels R&D-policy news network

Problem. Hundreds of source websites to monitor for relevant news; manual screening didn’t scale and important items were getting missed or delayed.

Approach. An on-demand harvesting pipeline scrapes every source (failing sites auto-retried on a second engine — nothing silently dropped), an LLM screens and scores each item against the client’s own editorial rubric, and a secure dashboard shows the ranked shortlist with one-click export and a full ledger of what was rejected and why.

Outcome. ~160 websites monitored per run, the editor’s entire workflow reduced to two buttons, 100% of items accounted for with a machine-readable reason. A self-hosted LLM option keeps data on the client’s own infrastructure.

Principle. Every rejection explained, human review for sensitive items, fully documented and handed over.
