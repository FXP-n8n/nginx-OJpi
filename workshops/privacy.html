Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Who We Are

4-Eyes provides AI literacy workshops and consulting services to European lobbying associations. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, organization, and phone number when you fill out our contact form or register for a workshop.
  • Communication data: The content of your messages to us and our responses.
  • Usage data: Basic website analytics (page views, referral sources) via privacy-compliant tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested information
  • Register you for workshops and send related communications
  • Send newsletters or updates (only if you have opted in)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: When you submit a form or opt in to communications
  • Contract performance: When you register for a workshop or engage our services
  • Legitimate interests: For website analytics and service improvement

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Typically:

  • Contact form submissions: 2 years
  • Workshop registrations: 3 years (for follow-up and records)
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Email delivery, website hosting, and form processing tools (all GDPR-compliant)
  • Legal authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at francois-xavier.peers@4-eyes.eu.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is processed within the European Union. If we use any service providers outside the EU, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

10. Analytics & Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our website. This service uses cookies and may process data such as your IP address (anonymized where possible), pages visited, device type, and referral source. We only load Google Analytics after you have given consent via our cookie banner. This processing is based on your consent under GDPR Article 6(1)(a). You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser’s local storage for this site.

For more information about how Google processes data, see the Google Privacy Policy.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
