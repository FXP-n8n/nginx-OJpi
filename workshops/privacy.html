What we collect

Contact-form submissions (name, organisation, email, message, topic) are sent directly to our own automation system, emailed to the owner, and used only to respond to your enquiry.

Analytics

We run our own first-party, cookieless pageview logging on our own infrastructure — no third-party tracker, no cookies, no visitor ID, no cross-site tracking. Each page load records only the page path, the referring page, and the browser's user-agent string. Because no cookie or persistent identifier is used, no cookie-consent banner is required.
