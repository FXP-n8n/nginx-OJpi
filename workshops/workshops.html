Intermediate Level

Advanced Prompt Engineering

Half-day (3–4 hours) · In-person or remote · Max 15 participants

Perfect for: Research teams, policy analysts, and briefing writers who use AI daily and want to master advanced techniques.

Prerequisites: AI Essentials or equivalent foundational knowledge.

What You'll Learn

  • Research acceleration — Query multiple sources simultaneously, synthesize findings, and extract key insights.
  • Document analysis at scale — Process 50+ page position papers, extract commitments, compare against voting records.
  • Fact-checking workflows — AI-assisted verification without over-reliance. Human-in-the-loop patterns.
  • Building reusable prompt libraries — Team-wide templates for consistent, high-quality outputs.

Workshop Outcomes

  • Advanced prompt patterns for complex research tasks
  • Document analysis workflow (PDF → structured insights)
  • Multi-source verification protocol
  • Team prompt library template (ready to customize)
Result: Reduce research time by 60% without losing analytical rigor.

Leadership Level

AI Systems Architecture

Half-day (3–4 hours) · In-person or remote · IT directors & operations leaders

Perfect for: IT directors, operations managers, and CTOs evaluating AI infrastructure investments for their organization.

What You'll Learn

  • Multi-agent architecture overview — How autonomous agent fleets work, when they're appropriate, and when they're overkill.
  • Verification protocols — Post-mortem loops, coherence checks, and failure detection systems.
  • Integration with existing workflows — Email, CRM, document management systems, and Google Workspace.
  • Risk registers & mitigation — Technical, compliance, and reputational risks with concrete mitigation strategies.

Workshop Outcomes

  • Multi-agent architecture decision framework
  • Verification protocol design patterns
  • Integration roadmap template
  • Risk register starter (with EU-specific considerations)
Result: A clear, actionable roadmap for AI infrastructure investment — or a confident decision to wait.
