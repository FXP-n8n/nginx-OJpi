About 4-Eyes

AI infrastructure for European lobbying associations — built on verification, not promises.

Why We Exist

European lobbying associations shape policy that affects millions. They deserve AI infrastructure that's as rigorous as the work they do.

We founded 4-Eyes because we saw a gap: most AI training is either too technical (focused on developers) or too superficial (focused on hype). Policy teams need something in between — practical, verification-first workflows that respect the stakes of advocacy work.

The 4-Eyes Principle: Every critical output is reviewed by at least two independent systems — human or AI. This isn't redundancy; it's rigor. In lobbying, a single hallucination can damage credibility with MEPs and Commission officials.

Our Principles

The beliefs that shape how we work.

  • Verification over promises. We don't sell AI as a magic solution. We build systems that catch errors before they become embarrassments.
  • EU-specific expertise. GDPR, compliance, and Brussels policy context aren't afterthoughts — they're built into every workshop and system.
  • Practical outcomes. Participants leave with prompts they'll use this week. Not theory — immediate time savings.
  • Transparency in failure. When AI systems fail (and they will), post-mortem reviews make them stronger. We publish anonymized failure analyses.
  • Human judgment, amplified. AI doesn't replace policy expertise — it amplifies it. The human stays in the loop.
